Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Answers to what patients commonly ask before their first visit. For anything specific to your test, call ahead.

Do I need a doctor's referral to get tested?
No — Medical Laboratory Services accepts both physician-referred and self-requested testing. If you have a referral, bring it with you; otherwise call ahead to discuss which tests are suitable.
Is walk-in testing available, or do I need an appointment?
Both branches accept bookings, and walk-ins may be seen depending on demand. Calling the Sliema branch ahead of time is the best way to confirm same-day availability at either location.
Will my results be shared with my doctor?
Yes. Results are issued to you directly, and where a referring physician is on file, they receive a copy as well.
Which branch should I visit — Sliema or Żejtun?
Both branches offer diagnostics and wellness screening. Sliema is the main branch, based at St. James Capua Hospital; Żejtun is the closer option for patients in the south of Malta. Call +356 2329 1604 if you're unsure which suits you best.
Do you accept health insurance?
See the Insurance & Health Funds section above — call the Sliema branch to confirm whether your specific plan is accepted.
Is this website accessible?
We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 AA. See our Accessibility Statement in the footer, or contact us if you hit a barrier.

Ready to book a test?

Speak to the Sliema branch to arrange testing at either location, ask about available panels, or check pricing.
